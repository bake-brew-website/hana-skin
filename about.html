Our Story

HANA SKIN was born from the quiet mornings of Kyoto, inspired by ancestral beauty rituals and the serene harmony between skin and nature. What began as small‑batch formulations crafted in a family atelier has blossomed into a global community that values simplicity, purity, and mindful self‑care.

Meet Our Founder

Aiko Nakamura grew up watching her grandmother infuse camellia oil and rice water into delicate skincare preparations. Guided by this legacy and a degree in cosmetic science, she created HANA SKIN to share those time‑honored Japanese ingredients—modernized with clean, gentle formulas. Every product is a reflection of Aiko’s belief: true beauty blooms in stillness and authenticity.
